About Vowy

Wedding planning without the spreadsheet sprawl.

Vowy is a collaborative wedding planning workspace built to help couples keep projects, vendors, tasks, and timelines in one place. It combines a visual board, structured planning, and shared access so the work stays clear instead of chaotic.

What Vowy does

  • Organizes wedding work into clear boards and lists.
  • Lets couples and collaborators coordinate in one shared project.
  • Tracks tasks, planning stages, and project details without switching tools.
  • Helps reduce duplicate work, missed steps, and scattered conversations.

Who it is for

Vowy is designed for engaged couples and anyone helping them plan, including family, wedding planners, and collaborators who need a simple place to stay aligned.

The product focuses on practical coordination first: fewer tabs, clearer ownership, and a better sense of what needs to happen next.

How it works

Users create a project, break work into categories and tasks, and collaborate inside the same planning space. Depending on the account plan, teams can unlock additional collaboration features and shared editing capabilities.

Who am I

Hi, I'm Debora. I got the idea for Vowy after seeing friends struggle to organize their weddings across too many messages, notes, and spreadsheets. I wanted to build something that could make the process feel more organized, less stressful, and a lot easier to share.

Vowy is a brand new product, and it's a one-person job from end to end. I'm still improving it constantly, and I genuinely hope it becomes useful for the people who try it.

If you like it, that's great. If you don't, or if something feels missing, I'm very open to feedback. The goal is to keep making Vowy better over time.
