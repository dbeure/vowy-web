Vowy Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Vowy collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you use the service. Vowy is operated by a sole proprietor based in Switzerland.

2. Data we collect

We may collect and process the following categories of data:

  • Account information such as your email address and authentication details
  • Project and task data that you create or upload in the app
  • Billing-related information handled through our payment providers
  • Technical information needed to operate and secure the service, such as basic device, browser, and log data

3. How we use data

We use personal data to:

  • Provide and maintain the Vowy service
  • Authenticate users and manage accounts
  • Process subscriptions and billing events
  • Respond to support, legal, and security matters
  • Improve the reliability and functionality of the product

4. Legal basis

Where applicable, we process personal data because it is necessary to provide the service, fulfill contractual obligations, comply with legal requirements, or pursue legitimate interests such as security, fraud prevention, and product improvement.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only where necessary with service providers that help operate Vowy, such as:

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers
  • Authentication providers
  • Payment processors

These providers may process data only as needed to support the service.

6. Data retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. If you request deletion, we will handle that request within a reasonable timeframe, subject to any legal retention requirements.

7. International processing

Depending on the infrastructure used to operate Vowy, your data may be processed outside Switzerland. Where this happens, we aim to use providers and safeguards appropriate for lawful data handling.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing

To exercise these rights, contact Vowy directly.

10. Children

Vowy is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to use the service independently. If you believe personal data has been provided unlawfully by a child, contact us so we can review and address it.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page, and continued use of Vowy after an update means the revised policy applies.

12. Contact

For privacy questions, support requests, or deletion requests, please contact us directly.
